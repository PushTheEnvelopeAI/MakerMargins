# Asset Provenance — Template Images

> Documents the source and licensing of all bundled template images in `MakerMargins/Assets.xcassets/`. Required for Apple's Content Rights Declaration in App Store Connect.

---

## Summary

- **Total template images:** 42
- **Source:** All 42 generated with **Google Gemini** (AI image generation)
- **License:** Google's Gemini Terms of Service grant the user rights to use, publish, and commercially distribute generated images. No royalty, no attribution required, no restriction on inclusion in paid apps.
- **Copyright note:** Per US Copyright Office guidance (2023-2025), AI-generated images are not eligible for copyright registration. This is a non-issue for bundled app assets — the images are decorative, not sold independently.
- **App Store compliance:** Content Rights Declaration in App Store Connect can truthfully state "I own or have obtained rights to all content." Google's ToS grant those rights.
